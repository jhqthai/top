Vietnamese Braised Fish

A simple traditional southern Vietnamese dish served with rice. Caremalised braised fish (Cá Kho) is a flavour bomb of savoury sweetness from a mix of fish sauce and sugar with aromatic tear inducing salty, bitter, sweet and spicy from spring onions, pepper and chilly. Cá Kho has a special characteristic where its flavours enhance after it is stored and recook the day after. This dish is a stable for busy Vietnamese mums. No cap.

Ingredients

  • 300g fish
Marinate ingredients
  • 1 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2/3 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon msg
  • 1 teaspoon caremal colouring (Optional)
Garnish ingredients
  • 1/2 bulb spring onion
  • 1/2 red Thai chilly (Optinal)

Vietnamese Braised Fish

This is a traditional crispy pork belly recipe modified to be cooked in an air-frier. The utisisation of an air-frier reduces cooking time while maintaining an excellent flavour, juiciness and crispiness.

Ingredients

  • 1kg pork belly
Meat side marinate ingredient
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon five spice powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1/2 teasspoon msg
Skin side marinate ingredient
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder or soda powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon rice wine or vinegar

Directions

Preparation
  1. Wipe moisture off the pork belly skin
  2. Poke holes in the skin for crispiness
  3. Slice the meat side till it reaches the fat and approximately 3cm apart
  4. Marinate the meat side first then the skin side
  5. Place the meat in an uncovered container and leave it in the fridge for a day
Cooking
  1. Place the pork belly meat side up in the air-frier for 20 minutes at 180℃
  2. After 20 minutes, flip the pork belly skin side up. Leave in air-frier for 15 minutes at 200℃
  3. Place the pork belly on a chopping board and let it rest for 5 to 10 minutes

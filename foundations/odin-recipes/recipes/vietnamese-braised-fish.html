Vietnamese Braised Fish (C치 Kho)

A simple traditional southern Vietnamese dish served with rice. Caremalised braised fish (C치 Kho) is a flavour bomb of savoury sweetness from a mix of fish sauce and sugar with aromatic tear inducing salty, bitter, sweet and spicy from spring onions, pepper and chilly. C치 Kho has a special characteristic where its flavours enhance after it is stored and recook the day after. This dish is a stable for busy Vietnamese mums. No cap.

Ingredients

300g fish
1/2 tablespoon cooking oil

Marinate ingredients

1 tablespoons fish sauce
2/3 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon msg
1 teaspoon caremal colouring (Optional)

Garnish ingredients

1/2 bulb spring onion
1/2 red Thai chilly (Optinal)

Directions

Preparation
  1.  Clean and portion the fish appropriately
  2.  Marinate the fish with marinate ingredients for 15 minutes
Cooking
  1.  Heat the pot up with oil then pour the fish in at high heat till boiled
  2.  Add 1/2 cup of water (or any stock) till boiled then set heat to low
  3.  Flip fish after 3 to 5 minutes
  4.  Cook till sauce is reduceda and thickened
  5.  Add ganish and serve

Vietnamese Caremalised Pork Belly (Thịt Kho)

A simple traditional Vietnamese dish. Some other description

Ingredients

1kg pork belly with more meat
8 eggs

Boil ingredients

1 liter of water
1 onion
1 onion skin
1/2 tablespoon salt

Fry ingredients (keep for simmer bag)

40 grams shallot
2 tablespoon oil
2 pieces of garlic

Marinate ingredients

1 tablespoon salt
1 tablespoon sugar
1 tablespoon chicken powder
2 tablespoons fish sauce
1 teaspoon msg
1 teaspoon caremal colouring (optional)

Simmer bag ingredients

1 teaspoon pepper
1 onion skin
2 pieces of garlic
1 chilli pepper
Fry ingredients

Soup ingredients

1/2 litre coconut juice
1 tablespoon chicken powder
1/2 tablespoon sugar
1 tablespoons fish sauce

Directions

Preparation
  1.  Clean the meat with hot water and salt
  2.  Quick char all side of the meat while pressing to achieve charness
  3.  Re-clean the skins with hot water (optional)
  4.  Boil 1 liter of water with the boil ingredients
  5.  Quick dip the meat in boiled water, remove when meat bounces back when touch
  6.  Portion the meat about 3-4cm lenth-width
  7.  Quick char fry ingredients and meat in medium-high heat pan
  8.  Add simmer bag ingredients into simmer bag
  9.  Marinate the meat with marinate ingredients and leave for 1 hour
Cooking
  1.  Add soup ingridents into pot and boil
  2.  Add meat and its marinate
  3.  When soup boil again, add coconut water to nearly cover the meat
  4.  Keep the pot going at low boil state with lid open
  5.  Remove scums
  6.  Add simmer bag
  7.  Boil eggs for around 10 minutes
  8.  After 10 minutes, close lid with slight open at low heat
  9.  Removes eggs shells and add eggs into pot
  10.  Continue the simmering process with low heat and slightly open lid
  11.  Every 15-30min remove scums (if exist) and flip eggs
  12.  Remove the simmer bag after 1 hour and 20 minutes
  13.  Add chilly (optional) and continue the simmer with no lid at low-medium heat for 2-3 minutes
  14.  Serve!
